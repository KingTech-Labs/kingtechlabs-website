Education
We don't just build tools. We teach.

Every app we build includes the knowledge to back it up. We explain the why, not just the what.

Here's what's built into FreedomFi:

🗺️
Financial Roadmap

A step-by-step guide to prioritizing your money: from building an emergency fund to maxing out retirement accounts. Built into FreedomFi, free for all users.

⚖️
Debt Strategies

Understand the Avalanche and Snowball payoff methods. FreedomFi explains both and lets you model which saves you the most money over time.

📊
Budgeting Frameworks

Learn the 50/30/20 rule, zero-based budgeting, and more. Our apps put financial education in context, right where you need it.

About
Who we are

KingTech Labs is a veteran owned & operated, independent iOS studio focused on building powerful, well-crafted apps with security built in from the ground up.

🔒
Built-In Security

We use encrypted connections and industry-standard security practices to protect your data. Every app we ship is built with the same rigor you'd expect from an enterprise product, by a CISSP-certified founder.

📚
Education First

We believe great tools come with great context. Every app we build includes built-in education to help you understand not just what to do, but why.

⚡
Indie & Innovative

Small studio, direct feedback loop. We move fast, innovate constantly, and ship improvements that matter.

CISSP Certified
