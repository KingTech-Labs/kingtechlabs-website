Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

FreedomFi is built on a simple principle: your financial data belongs to you. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or financial information on external servers.

Data Storage

All data you enter into FreedomFi (expenses, budgets, debts, goals, savings, and net worth) is stored exclusively on your device using your platform's secure local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

Biometric Authentication

FreedomFi offers an optional biometric lock feature that is off by default. When enabled, the app uses your device's built-in biometric authentication system (such as Face ID or Touch ID on iOS, or fingerprint authentication on Android) to protect access to the app.

We do not access, collect, or store any biometric data. Authentication is performed entirely by your device's operating system through its secure enclave. Your biometric data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Notifications

If you grant notification permissions, FreedomFi schedules local notifications on your device to remind you of upcoming bill due dates. These notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device. No notification content is sent to or processed by any external server.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, address)
  • No financial account credentials
  • No biometric data
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No crash reports sent to third-party services
  • No advertising identifiers

No Accounts Required

FreedomFi does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app. You can use all features anonymously.

Data Export and Deletion

You can export all of your data at any time from Settings as a JSON file. To delete all data, simply delete the app from your device. All locally stored data will be permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

FreedomFi is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

No Financial Advice

FreedomFi is for informational purposes only and does not constitute financial, tax, or investment advice. The content provided within the app, including educational materials, financial roadmaps, and contribution limit figures, is intended for general informational use only. Consult a qualified financial advisor before making financial decisions.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@kingtechlabs.com.
