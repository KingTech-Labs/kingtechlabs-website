The Story

"Malachar the Undying waits in his Citadel. Whether Ashwynd survives depends on how you get there."

The kingdom is fractured. Old alliances have crumbled. Dark forces gather at the borders while political rivals scheme from within. You have been thrust into power at the worst possible moment. Build your court, earn the trust of the factions, and prepare for a war that will define the realm. There is no single path to the Citadel. The story you write depends entirely on the choices you make along the way.
